Humanitarian Toolbox

When disaster strikes, code saves lives.

Mission

We connect people like you with organizations in need of your expertise. You can build the next generation of life-saving tools and refine them to meet new needs as they arise. We maintain and deploy this ‘toolbox’ of solutions quickly to get them in the hands of those making a difference. Everything we build is open-source and part of an open ecosystem so the tools can be adapted, integrated and deployed to particular situations as they evolve.

Please join us - regardless of your experience in open source or whether you are or want to be a coder, tester, designer, or something else - your skills can save lives!

How We Work

Creating Solutions

Humanitarian Toolbox is a sustained effort to leverage technology and skilled volunteer communities to solve the needs of humanitarian and response organizations and communities affected by disasters.

Building Together

We strive to bring together the greatest diversity of contributors (across experience levels, opportunity, and disciplines) to grow their own skills while they design, code, test and deploy solutions for those affected by disasters.

Delivering Impact

Our "toolbox" of open solutions can enable responders and organizations to deliver humanitarian relief more efficiently and grow communities' access to information and resiliency as they prepare for, recover from and rebuild after disasters.
